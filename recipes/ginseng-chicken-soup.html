Ginseng Chicken Soup


Image Credits

Description:

This ginseng chicken soup is so healthy. Drinking it will make you live up to 137 years old.

The legend says that if you prepare this soup with the rarest ginseng in the world (which will glow in the dark),

drinking it will turn you into Son Goku, and you will be able to use the ability called "Kamekameha"

Ingredients:

  • Ginseng
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Chicken
  • More ginseng
  • and some love

Steps:

  1. Play the flute and let the chicken dance for awhile to improve the texture of the meat
  2. Command the chicken to throw the ginseng into the pot
  3. Command the chicken to dive into the pot
  4. Add some salt and fill the pot with water until it barely surpase the height of the chicken
  5. Add the legendary glowy ginseng, if you have
  6. Watch Gordon Ramsey show, and wait for 30 minutes
  7. The soup is ready to be serve! Drink and live until age 137!
